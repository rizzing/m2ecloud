FAQ

How to list on eBay Motors?
Use eBay Motors parts listing software like M2E Cloud to list your vehicles and compatible parts/accessories. You only need to register an eBay account and connect it to M2E Cloud. Once you add your products to the Catalog, you’ll be able to create listings with your vehicles and compatible parts on eBay in four quick steps.
How to sell a vehicle on eBay?
To list a vehicle, you need to create an eBay account, click on the “Sell” button on the site, provide details such as make and model, upload images, set a price, and include any relevant information about the vehicle's condition, history, and features. Or try M2E Cloud, a dedicated listing tool that helps upload your inventory, including vehicles and their parts, on eBay and other channels.
Is eBay Motors different from eBay?
Yes, eBay Motors is a separate marketplace within eBay that is dedicated to buying and selling vehicles, including cars, trucks, motorcycles, and boats. It has specific features and tools designed for the automotive industry, such as vehicle history reports, financing options, and shipping services. While eBay Motors is part of eBay, it operates as a distinct platform with its own set of policies and guidelines.
How do I sell on eBay Motors with Shopify?
M2E Cloud integrates eBay Motors with your Shopify storefront, connects and synchronizes listings and orders. The app supports eBay Parts Compatibility, so you can handle eBay Motors-specific listings and create new ones without hassle. Once a sale is made on eBay Motors, M2E Cloud automatically syncs order details with your Shopify store.
Can I sell Amazon products through eBay Motors?
If you are selling vehicles and their parts/accessories on Amazon, you can upload them for sale on eBay Motors through M2E Cloud. Your only task is to connect your eBay and Amazon accounts, add products to Catalog, and create eBay listings based on your Amazon ones.
