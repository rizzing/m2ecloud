FAQ

How to link my website to my eBay store?
One of the easiest ways to link the eBay store with your website is to use an all-in-one automated solution linking eBay to the most prominent e-commerce sites. Shopify users can take advantage of the M2E eBay App, an independent application that links and synchronizes inventories from the store and eBay to drive further sales.
I'm a new seller on eBay. Does your solution fit me?
M2E eBay App for Shopify is suitable for aspiring eBay merchants. If you are just starting out, take advantage of a free trial to explore the solution, its feature set and see whether it aligns with your specific needs. Our team provides expert customer service and comprehensive support resources to ensure you have all the guidance you need as a new seller.
Do you have software to analyze my eBay sales?
We have a dedicated M2E Analytics tool that offers a comprehensive overview of eBay sales metrics. The tool provides detailed reports along with interactive charts that unlock insights into eBay operations, including overall performance, customer base, and success of your items. The tool is free to try out if you are an M2E eBay App user.
