Use case

The problem

Updating and synchronizing product details, inventory, and orders manually between Amazon and eBay using Ecwid

The solution

M2E Cloud software seamlessly automates the synchronization of order details and product information, including titles, descriptions, images, quantities, and prices, across the Ecwid store, Amazon, and eBay. Any modifications made on one platform are instantly mirrored in the others, guaranteeing uniform and current product information.

FAQ

How to connect Ecwid to Amazon?
To seamlessly integrate Ecwid with Amazon, consider employing a specialized extension such as M2E Cloud. It facilitates the connection between your Ecwid store and Amazon's services, offering features such as automated order fulfillment and inventory synchronization. Simply register for an M2E Cloud account, follow the setup instructions, and configure the required settings to establish a smooth and efficient link between your Ecwid store and Amazon's platform.
How to sync Ecwid with eBay?
M2E Cloud Ecwid eBay integration simplifies the process by enabling you to list Ecwid items on eBay and, conversely, manage inventory and orders through a user-friendly interface. All you have to do is follow the setup instructions, link your Ecwid and eBay profiles, and activate automatic item linking and synchronization.
