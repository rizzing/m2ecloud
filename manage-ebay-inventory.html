FAQ

How to keep track of eBay inventory?
Consider using eBay’s built-in tools or specialized software for managing eBay inventory, like M2E Cloud. The main benefit of the application is automated, productive, and live inventory tracking, along with centralized stock control, regardless of your inventory size. With such an application at your disposal, there is no need to spend hours creating spreadsheets or databases and manually tracking your items.
What methods can eBay sellers use to control and avoid out-of-stock issues?
Start with investing in the eBay inventory app that offers live tracking and automated stock level updates. One of the most prominent options is M2E Cloud, which monitors and refreshes inventory levels on eBay 24/7 without having to conduct routine physical stock audits to verify its accuracy.
Another working method is establishing reorder points for your items to prompt restocking when inventory reaches a particular threshold, ensuring you replenish stock before running out.
What advantages do barcoding or SKU systems offer in an eBay inventory setup?
By establishing a barcoding or SKU system, you’ll need to assign unique identifiers to each eBay item. This ensures the precise identification of items, reducing the likelihood of errors in tracking and handling inventory, whether you do it manually or rely on eBay inventory software. These systems also facilitate live inventory tracking, helping eBay merchants keep stock levels precise and up-to-date.
